Meatloaf

This is what meatloaf looks like!!!!!!!

Meatloaf is really good. Food is pretty good, so how can meatloaf be bad? It's really easy to cook, too, so why not?

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onions
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Get ground beef!
  2. Season it with salt and pepper!
  3. Chop onions and throw them in
  4. Put meatloaf in oven
  5. Wait until it is ready.
  6. Eat away!!!!!!
