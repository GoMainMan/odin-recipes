Lasagna

This is what your final project will look like!

Lasagna is really good. We will be making one using few ingredients - who wants to use the whole kitchen??

Ingredients

  • Italian Sausage
  • Lean Ground Beef
  • Minced Onion
  • Garlic
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • White Sugar
  • Fennel Seeds
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Egg
  • Cheese
  • Noodles

Steps

  1. Get all ingredients together
  2. Stick it in the oven
  3. Make sure it does not burn
  4. Pull it out and enjoy!!!!
