Toast

This is what your final step will look like!!!!

Toast is very simple to make. It's great to have if you're in a rush or are feeling lazy for the day. All you need is bread and some butter!

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Get bread
  2. Stick it in the toaster
  3. Don't burn it
  4. Pull it out and put on some butter
  5. Eat it!!!!!
